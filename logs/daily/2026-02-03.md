## Work Completed

### Session 1: Brainstorm - Rijksuitgaven Reporter (V2)

**Cofounder input:** "Een agent die 24/7 het nieuws scant en dat koppelt aan ontvangers en uitgaven, de Rijksuitgaven Reporter, die afhankelijk van je beroep die data omzet in info waar je direct wat mee kunt."

**Key design decisions:**

| Decision | Choice | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Classification approach | Classify news, not recipients | Recipients hard to classify; news has context |
| News sources | Free RSS (Rijksoverheid, Kamerstukken, NOS, RTL) | Start simple, ~50 articles/day |
| Output format | Daily email digest | High engagement, simpler than in-app |
| Delivery time | Midday (12:00-12:30) | Captures morning + overnight news |
| Personalization | 2 profession templates | Journalist, Beleidsmedewerker |
| AI content level | Light analysis | Data + insights, build trust first |
| Items per email | 3-5 focused | Quality over quantity |
| Email platform | Resend | Modern, good deliverability, future-proof |
| Signup | Platform opt-in | Keep it simple |

**Roadmap update:**
- Reporter becomes **V2** (was: Theme Discovery)
- Theme Discovery shifts to **V3**
- All subsequent versions shift by one (V3â†’V4, etc.)
- Reporter can launch after V1 without IBOS themes
